World’s Largest Sea Beach

Cox’s Bazaar

Cox's Bazar (Bengali:কক্সবাজার Kôks bajar) is a town, a fishing port and district headquarters in Bangladesh. The beach in Cox's Bazar is an unbroken 125 km sea beach with a gentle slope, one of the world's longest. It is located 150 km south of the industrial port Chittagong
